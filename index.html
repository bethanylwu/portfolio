Hey! I'm Bethany. You may also know me as B!
I am a New York-based designer and mixed media artist whose work explores intangible elements—light, sound, reflection, and time—through material experimentation and perceptual manipulation.

My childhood habit of re-arranging my shared bedroom, much to my sister's annoyance, sparked my pursuit of a Bachelor's Degree in Interior Design and Technical Theatre specializing in Scenic and Properties Design.
Currently, I am a Master's student at the Interactive Telecommunications Program (ITP) at NYU Tisch School of the Arts.

While still rooted in physical objects, my recent practice incorporates digital and computational elements to expand interactive possibilities. My work is often an invitation to slow down and reconnect with the value of handmade craft. Through a blend of digital innovation and hands-on techniques I aim to celebrate the care and skill that often gets overlooked in our fast-paced world.

2025
Flocking | Dumbo Projection Project, Vol. Six
Projection Art
Co-Creator in collaboration with Audrey Oh and Sai Ram Ved Vijapurapu
.
"Flocking" is a site-specific projection artwork created for the DUMBO Projection Project. The installation explores the intersection between industrial infrastructure and the natural ecosystem vital to the area. Inspired by the site's distinctive soundscape, we anchored our creative process in the auditory experience, translating it into visual form through responsive animated birds that dynamically reacts to the loud presence of passing trains.

2025
Reveal, Generate, Blend | NYU ITP Spring Show 2025
Exhibition Design
Co-Creator in collaboration with Sky Seo
.
“Reveal, Generate, Blend” is a playful and educational activity based exhibit about additive color - also known as color mixing with light. Through playful interactions, visitors will learn about additive color, make creative decisions, and generate their own unique light painting. This exhibit encourages exploration to deepen existing understanding of color and expand knowledge of light.

2024
Where You Linger | NYU ITP Winter Show 2024
Interactive Art
.
"Where You Linger" is an interactive diorama that explores our personal relationships with time when we are engaged with our work. Inside the diorama, which the user sees through a peephole, is a Pepper's Ghost projection of a person engaging in different activities at a desk. The user is able to alter the passage of time within the diorama, which reflects how fleeting time can be when we are focused on a task at hand. This creates a different experience for each user depending on where they choose to linger in the model.

2022
Yoseka Ceramics Watercolors | Yoseka Stationery
Product
Designer and creator of custom watercolor paints for Yoseka Stationery
.
The Yoseka Ceramic Watercolors are blends of multiple pigments to achieve a close color match with the Yoseka Ceramic Fountain pen inks. All pigments are premixed into individual batches and then blended together at their designated ratios, mulled again to ensure fully mixed, and finally set into watercolor pans for drying and packaging.
